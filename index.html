About


Maxcel Trading is authorized distributor of Sumitomo Products in Mindanao.
Our company is engaged in providing highest quality power transmission products, solutions for Fluid Sealing and
Control System needs. Our aim is to serve our clients with high quality and reliable products such as Gearboxs, Gearmotors,
Frequency Inverter, Flowmeters, Flow Switches, Control Valves, Ball Valves, Butterfly Valves, Actuators, Pressure Gauge,
Pressure Transmitters & Switches, Temperature Measuring Instruments, Mechanical Seal, O-rings, pH, Conductivity
and other Instrumentation Products.
